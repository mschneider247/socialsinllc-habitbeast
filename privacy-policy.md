## 1. Introduction

HabitBeast ("the App") is operated by Social Sin LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use HabitBeast.

By using HabitBeast, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2a. Information You Provide
- **Email address** — used for account creation and authentication
- **Monster name** — the name you choose for your in-game monster (not required to be a real name)
- **Habit data** — habits you select and track (habit types, completion status, streak counts, tier progression)
- **Battle data** — battle actions earned, challenge history, win/loss records

### 2b. Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage data** — app screens visited, features used, session duration (via Firebase Analytics)
- **Crash reports** — error logs and stack traces when the app crashes (via Firebase Crashlytics)
- **Device information** — device type, operating system version, app version (for crash diagnostics only)

### 2c. Information We Do NOT Collect
- Real name, physical address, or phone number
- Location data
- Contacts or calendar data
- Photos or camera data
- Financial information (payment is handled entirely by the App Store/Play Store)

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

| Purpose | Data Used |
|---------|-----------|
| Account authentication and security | Email address |
| Providing game features (habits, battles, leveling) | Habit data, battle data |
| Displaying leaderboards (opt-in only) | Monster name, level |
| Fixing crashes and bugs | Crash reports, device info |
| Understanding how the app is used | Anonymized usage data |

We do **not** sell your personal information to third parties.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored in **Firebase (Google Cloud)** in the `us-central1` region
- Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest (Firebase default encryption)
- Access to your data is controlled by Firebase Security Rules — only you can access your own habit and battle data

## 6. Leaderboards

If you choose to appear on the leaderboard (opt-in setting), your **monster name** and **level** will be visible to other players. Your email address is never visible to other players.

You can opt out of the leaderboard at any time in your Profile settings.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

- **Access** — request a copy of your personal data
- **Deletion** — delete your account and all associated data
- **Correction** — update inaccurate information in your profile
- **Opt-out** — disable analytics tracking or leaderboard visibility

### Deleting Your Account
To delete your account and all associated data:
1. Open HabitBeast
2. Go to Profile → Settings
3. Tap "Delete Account"

Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request.

Alternatively, email us at **privacy@socialsin.co** with subject line "Delete My Account."

---

## 9. Data Retention

- **Active accounts:** Data retained while your account is active
- **Deleted accounts:** All personal data deleted within 30 days
- **Anonymized analytics:** Aggregated, non-identifiable data may be retained indefinitely
